About Us

We are the music club of IIIT Delhi. In the middle of assignements and deadlines and what not, we are a community of musicians who survive our musical life by conducting regular talent nights, karaoke nights, meet-ups, theory talks and bonfires

We are responsible for sending students out for competitions in various college festivals around India and participate as much as we can so that the other colleges know about our existance. We aim to learn as much as we can from each other and our experiences

We are also responsible to perform in the various events that happen within IIIT Delhi inlcluding Republic Day, Independance Day, Convocation Day, Alumni Meets, etc.
