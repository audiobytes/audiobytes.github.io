About Us

We are the music club of IIIT Delhi. In the middle of assignements and deadlines and what not, we are a community of musicians who survive our musical life by conducting regular talent nights, karaoke nights, meet-ups, theory talks and bonfires.

We are responsible for sending students out for competitions in various college festivals around India and participate as much as we can. We aim to learn as much as we can from each other and our experiences.

We also perform in the various events that happen within IIIT Delhi including Republic Day, Independence Day, Convocation Day, Alumni Meets, etc.
